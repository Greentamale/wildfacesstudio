Meet the Artist

Welcome to Wild Faces Studio, where imagination takes form and spirit animals come to life. Each mask is lovingly handcrafted, painted, and detailed to capture not just an animal's appearance, but its very essence.

What started as a creative passion has blossomed into wearable art that helps people transform, express themselves, and connect with their wild side. Whether you're heading to a convention, performing on stage, or simply embracing your inner creature, there's a Wild Face waiting for you.

Each mask is crafted from high-quality, lightweight materials designed for comfort during extended wear. The base is sculpted paper pulp which is then painted by hand, and augmented with faux fur accents and mesh eye openings for visibility. Masks with extended ears and/or snouts use an additional cardboard base. Specific materials may vary by design to achieve the perfect look and feel for each creature.
